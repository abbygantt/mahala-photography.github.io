Hi, I'm Autumn Mahala and here's my story! I am a photographer from Statesville, North Carolina who focuses mainly on landscapes and portraits, specifically fashion and beauty.My love for photography began when I was young. I am self-taught and have devoted several hours learning how use a camera and capture a "great" photo. I am also certied in Adobe Photoshop. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me redecorating, spending time with my family or scouting for new locations to shoot at. I would describe my photography style as creative and storytelling. During a session, I do encourage some posing and direction, but for the most part I like to keep it natural. Being able to capture an image that makes someone truly feel beautiful and confident is my goal. I like for my images to tell a story on their own and for you to feel like you are there in that moment. Book with me, and let me help you tell your story!
